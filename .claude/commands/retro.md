# /retro — retrospective

Capture what to keep and what to improve — for the product and for the team itself (continuous improvement).

## Steps

1. Reflect: what went well, what to improve, what surprised us.
2. Write it to `scrum/retros/retro-NN.md` (matching the sprint number) as plain markdown — no telemetry, no external calls.
3. Split improvements into **product** (→ new or updated `scrum/backlog.md` items) and **team/agent** improvements.
4. For team/agent improvements, load `self-improving-agent`: turn each learning into a concrete edit — a `/specialize <agent>` run, a `catalog/` edit, or a skill change — and log the change in the retro file.
5. Keep changes small and evidence-based (KISS, Surgical Changes).
